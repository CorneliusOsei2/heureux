### **Passage**

```
Je suis dans le metro, j'arrive dans 20 minutes au bureau. Commencez la
reunion de travail sans moi. Merci. Jean.
(I'm on the metro, I'll arrive at the office in 20 minutes. Start the
work meeting without me. Thanks. Jean.)
```

### **Prompt & Answers**

|  | French | English |
| --- | --- | --- |

| **B** | **Ses collegues** | **His colleagues** |
| C | Ses enfants | His children |
| D | Ses voisins | His neighbors |

### **Correct Answer: B -- Ses collegues**

"Bureau" (office) and "reunion de travail" (work meeting) are professional vocabulary. The message asks recipients to start a work meeting -- something only colleagues would do. The workplace context is unmistakable.

### **Why the others are wrong**

**A -- Ses amis.** Friends don't hold "reunions de travail" (work meetings) at a "bureau" (office).

**C -- Ses enfants.** Children don't attend work meetings. The tone is professional, not familial.

**D -- Ses voisins.** Neighbors have no connection to office meetings.
