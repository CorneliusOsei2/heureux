## **Q1**

### **Passage**

```
Paul,
M. Redon a téléphoné. Il ne vient pas aujourd'hui
car il a la grippe.

(Paul,
Mr. Redon called. He is not coming today
because he has the flu.)
```

### **Prompt & Answers**

|  | French | English |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Prompt** | Pourquoi est-ce que M. Redon annule son rendez-vous ? | Why is Mr. Redon cancelling his appointment? |
| A | Il a perdu ses papiers | He lost his papers |
| B | Il a trop de travail | He has too much work |
| **C** | **Il est très malade** | **He is very sick** |
| D | Il part en vacances | He is going on vacation |

### **Correct Answer: C -- Il est très malade**

The message states M. Redon "ne vient pas aujourd'hui car il a la grippe" (is not coming today because he has the flu). The flu (la grippe) is an illness, so he is very sick. "Il est très malade" is a direct restatement of the reason given: having the flu means being sick.

### **Why the others are wrong**

**A -- Il a perdu ses papiers.** Nothing in the message mentions lost papers. The sole reason given is illness.

**B -- Il a trop de travail.** Work overload is not mentioned. The cancellation is clearly attributed to the flu.

**D -- Il part en vacances.** There is no mention of a vacation. He is cancelling because of a health problem, not travel plans.
