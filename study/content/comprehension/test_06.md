**C -- Valeurs partagées par une élite.** No mention of an elite. The French spirit is presented as a national characteristic, not an elite trait.

**D -- Aptitude à l'ouverture.** The text describes contradictions and tensions, not openness. The spirit is about opposing qualities, not open-mindedness.
